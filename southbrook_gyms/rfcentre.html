What is Rangiora Fitness

Rangiora Fitness Centre is a welcoming, community-focused gym designed to help you reach your health and fitness goals. Whether you are just a hopeless high school student or a body builder, Rangiora Fitness Centre provides the space, equipment, and the overall support you need to succeed and reach your goals. Our gym features high-quality cardio and strength machines, a functional training area, and a variety of group fitness classes such as HIIT and REVT, all led by experienced and motivating instructors. If you want more personalized guidance, our certified personal trainers are available to create tailored workout plans to help you track your health & fitness goals.
At Rangiora Fitness Centre, we believe fitness should be enjoyable, motivating, and inclusive for all. That is why we focus on building a friendly environment where everyone feels comfortable and supported. Our staff are approachable, the facility is clean and safe perfect for your next workout. We also know that life can be busy. That is why we offer you a flexible membership, convenient opening hours that are 24/7, and a local approach. Whether you want to build strength, improve endurance, lose weight, or simply feel better about yourself, Rangiora Fitness Centre is here to support you every step of your journey.
