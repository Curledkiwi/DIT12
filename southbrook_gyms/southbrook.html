What is Southbrook?

Southbrook is a small suburb located in the south of Rangiora, within the Waimakariri District Positioned about 30 minutes north of the biggest city in South Island being Christchurch, Southbrook has slowly transformed from a semi-rural area into a growing residential and light industrial area. The area has a blend of housing, local businesses, and community facilities, including schools (high school and primary school) and some sports facilities, catering to everyone. With ongoing development, Southbrook contributes to the wider growth of the Waimakariri District.
