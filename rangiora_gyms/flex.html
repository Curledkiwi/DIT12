What is Flex Fitness

Flex Fitness is a premium, 24/7 gym brand built for those who are dead serious about achieving their own fitness goals, whether you're just starting out or pushing your performance to another level. With a strong focus on community, results, and the best motivation, Flex Fitness offers the environment, equipment, and support you need to train your way for your needs. Our clubs feature top-of-the-line strength and cardio equipment, free weights, and recovery tools like a sauna and a cold plung. Many locations also include group training classes, personal training to help you be yourself.
At Flex Fitness, we believe training should be challenging, and inspiring. That’s why at our gyms we have the right energy and attitude, a no ego culture and motivating music. A perfect combo so that it encourages every member to push their limits. We’re open 24/7 so you can train when it suits you. With a few flexible membership options, access to multiple Flex locations across New Zealand. Whether your goal is to build muscle, get bigger, or simply feel better, Flex Fitness can help you every step of the way.
