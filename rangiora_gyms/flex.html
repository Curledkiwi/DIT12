What is Stadium Fitness

Flex Fitness is a premium, 24/7 gym brand built for those who are serious about achieving their fitness goals — whether you're just starting out or pushing your performance to the next level. With a strong focus on community, results, and motivation, Flex Fitness offers the environment, equipment, and support you need to train your way. Our clubs feature top-of-the-line strength and cardio equipment, free weights, functional training zones, and recovery tools. Many locations also include group training classes, personal training, and exclusive performance gear to keep you moving and progressing.
At Flex Fitness, we believe training should be empowering, challenging, and inspiring. That’s why our gyms are designed with energy and attitude — from bold interiors and motivating music to a supportive, no-ego culture that encourages every member to push their limits. We’re open 24/7 so you can train when it suits you. With flexible membership options, access to multiple Flex locations across New Zealand, and a community of like-minded members, everything you need is at your fingertips. Whether your goal is to build muscle, get lean, improve performance, or simply feel better — Flex Fitness gives you the space, tools, and drive to make it happen.
