What is Kaiapoi Fitness

Kaiapoi Fitness Centre is a welcoming gym designed to help you achieve your personal goals. No matter where you come from. Whether you’re new to exercise or already on your journey to become yourself, we offer the space and perfect motivation you need to succeed in your goals. Our gym features high-quality cardio and strength equipment, and a range of group fitness classes all led by knowledgeable and energetic instructors. Looking for that extra support? Our personal trainers are here to create custom programmes tailored to your needs and wellbeing goals.
We believe fitness should be empowering, inclusive, and enjoyable. That is why we have a supportive environment where everyone that enters our door feels welcome and included. With a space filled with equipment to assist you in your fitness needs. From free weights to cardio machines, we have everything at your finger tips. We know how busy our lives can get. That is why we offer a flexible membership optionwith a community-first approach. Whether you are aiming to build strength or loosing weight we are with you on every step of your way.
