What is this website about?

This website is about all the different gyms in the Waimakariri Area. All in different catagories depending on the three locations that you are able to see at the top. All gyms are rated and described to find your best needs
Have fun exploring!

How are these gyms rated?

These gyms are rated on different aspects that you may want in a gym. Ranging from the following:
  • Price
  • Population
  • Location
  • Facility
